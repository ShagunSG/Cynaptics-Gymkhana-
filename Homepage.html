OUR TEAMS

Event Team Project Team Outreach Team
Event team looks after the organisation of all events we conduct. They are also responsible for the conduction of the monthly intra-club ML discussion sessions. The Projects Team does all the project-related tasks and assists in providing the Problem Statements (PS) for various events. The team also maintains the Club's blog on various ML topics. Outreach Team plays a key role in any external collaboration. They also maintain all social media handles of the club, including Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Discord, YouTube.
